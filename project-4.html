Mushroom Poisoning Predictions — Queens College: Applied Data Science

Deployed Python, sci-kit learn and pandas to preprocess a 1988 mushroom dataset and train random forest, GNB matrix, and SVM models to find the best model and parameters to predict the most poisonous species. Delivered a presentation to explain how my best model (the random forest) could be utilized for daily research into the usability of mushroom species for practical purposes.
