Store Simulation — Queens College: Software Engineering

Captained a six-member team to develop a store simulation in Java in the agile approach, where one could log on and play the role of either a boss, an employee, or a customer. Leading a series of weekly coding sessions on Replit, delineated roles, designed and programmed over 30 classes, implemented design patterns for coding efficiency, and documented the project extensively.
